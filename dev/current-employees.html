State of Florida Annual Open Enrollment for 2012

Important November 7 - November 18, 2011

Open Enrollment takes place each fall to allow eligible employees—excepting OPS employees—to make changes to their insurance plans for the upcoming year. All changes will be effective the following January and must be done through People First. You can contact them by calling 1-866-663-4735 or visiting their website at peoplefirst.myflorida.com.

How to Enroll or Make Changes

  • Visit the People First website at peoplefirst.myflorida.com (available 24/7)
  • Speak to a People First representative at 1-866-663-4735 (866-221-0268 TTY users) Monday - Friday between 8:00 a.m. and 6 p.m
  • Complete a paper enrollment and submit to People First directly by mailing or by fax 1-800-422-3128—be sure to make a copy for yourself
  You will need your People First user ID and password to access your enrollment account. If you don't have them, contact People First after reviewing the People First password guidelines.  

Need help using People First's website?

Visit hr.ucf.edu/web/benefits/People_First for a PDF that shows you how to enroll on the website. If you need additional instructions, training videos are available to help you.
